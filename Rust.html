Rust

  • When it was created?

    • Rust was created in 2006, Sponsored in 2009, and officially released in 2015.

  • Who created it (person or group)?

    • Rust was created by Graydon Hoare at Mozilla Research who would later sponsor the language.

  • General history of the language (how has it evolved over time)

    • Rust was originally a personal project of Grayson Hoare while he was working at Mozilla Research. Mozilla would later sponsor his project as part of the development of an experimental browser engine. The first stable version wasn't released until 2015 though there had been previous versions. During the pandemic Mozilla layed off many people and this led to the creation of the Rust Foundation, who wanted to take ownership of all the trademarks and domain names. The formantion of the foundation was announced in 2021 by its five founding compaines: AWS, Huawei, Google, Microsoft, and Mozilla.

  • What type of problems the language is typically used for.

    • Rust is typically used to develop applications that require scalability. This is due to Rust's ability to enhance safety, performance, and memory.

  • What is the current state of the language (who is still using it - what for?)

    • Rust seems to be a rather popular language to use due to its safety, performance, and memory. Rust was used by Discord to speed up its system and the language is getting more popular.
